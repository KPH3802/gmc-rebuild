## Closed Inputs / Outputs

- **Input:** exactly one positional argument `result`, a merged P6-09
  `DryRunReconciliationResult`. No `SimulatedPortfolio`, no `ExpectedPositions`,
  no `ReconciliationStatus`, no `SafetyVerdict`, no `DailyReport`, no raw config,
  no env var, no filesystem path, no clock value. A wrong-typed argument raises
  `TypeError`.
- **Output:** a plain JSON-serializable `dict` whose every leaf is a `str` or an
  `int`. `json.dumps(payload)` succeeds with no custom encoder. The
  `reconciliation_status` is echoed verbatim from the input result, preserving
  the ADR-003 `UNAVAILABLE` vs `FAILED` distinction. Every list preserves the
  canonical symbol-ascending order already enforced by the P6-09 result; the
  builder re-sorts nothing and mutates nothing.
