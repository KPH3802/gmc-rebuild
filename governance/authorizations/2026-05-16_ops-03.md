# OPS-03 Authorization — X10 Pro Panic-Investigation Record

Date: 2026-05-16
Authorizer: Kevin Heaney
Scope: Bounded, docs-only operations packet for `gmc-rebuild` documenting the read-only Seagate X10 Pro panic-history investigation performed on 2026-05-16. Records that the 2026-05-13 19:43 CDT reboot was ungraceful but, by the available 14-day read-only evidence, is **not directly tied** to the Seagate X10 Pro; that the stronger evidence pattern points to memory pressure plus a UI hang followed by a hard restart; that the X10 Pro remains acceptable in its current Backblaze-included online role; that the X10 Pro is **not** promoted to Layer 5 (offline / rotating cold copy) by this packet; and that the remaining X10 Pro gaps (no SMART visibility through the USB enclosure, `Owners: Disabled`, FileVault not enabled, and only 14 days of clean log evidence) are recorded. Records the recommended next gates before any future Layer 5 promotion (30-day clean observation window, SMART-visibility decision, encryption / ownership decision, and a cold restore drill). Adds a new `RECOVERY.md` §5.6 "Recorded Investigations" subsection with the dated 2026-05-16 investigation entry, makes conservative reflections in `RECOVERY.md` §8, §9.4, and §10, and adds this governance authorization artifact. Makes no other changes.
